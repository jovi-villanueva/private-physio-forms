1 Consent to Physiotherapy Treatment Required

I consent to physiotherapy assessment and treatment as recommended by my treating physiotherapist. I understand that:

  • Treatment may include manual therapy, exercise prescription, dry needling, taping, and other evidence-based interventions
  • The physiotherapist will explain each treatment and its risks before proceeding
  • I have the right to refuse or withdraw consent for any treatment at any time
  • Results cannot be guaranteed and outcomes vary between individuals
2 Privacy and Data Collection Required

I acknowledge and consent to the following:

  • My personal and health information will be collected and stored securely for the purpose of providing physiotherapy care
  • My information will not be shared with third parties without my consent, except where required by law or for direct referral purposes (e.g., GP correspondence)
  • I may request access to my health records at any time
  • Records will be retained for a minimum of 7 years from the date of last service (or until age 25 for minors), in accordance with NSW legislation
  • In the event of a data breach likely to cause serious harm, I will be notified along with the OAIC and/or NSW IPC as required by law
  • A full privacy policy is available on request and explains my rights in detail

Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) - APPs 3, 6, 11, 12, 13; Health Records and Information Privacy Act 2002 (NSW) - HPPs 1, 5, 7, 10, 11

3 AI-Assisted Documentation Optional

Artificial intelligence tools (Large Language Models) may be used to assist with clinical documentation. Please read the following and indicate your consent below:

  • AI tools assist with generating clinical notes (SOAP notes), structuring treatment plans, and organising progress data
  • AI tools do NOT make clinical decisions - all clinical judgement is made by the treating physiotherapist
  • AI-generated content is always reviewed and verified by your physiotherapist before being finalised
  • Only de-identified or minimally necessary clinical information is processed through AI tools
  • AI tools do not permanently retain or learn from your individual health data
  • AI processing may occur on servers outside Australia - data is encrypted in transit and not stored permanently
I consent to AI tools being used to assist with my clinical documentation
I do not consent to AI tools being used - I request manual documentation only

You may change this preference at any time. Opting out will not affect the quality of your care.

4 Audio Recording of Sessions Optional

With your consent, sessions may be audio recorded to assist with accurate clinical documentation:

  • Recordings are used to generate clinical notes (SOAP notes) to ensure accurate documentation
  • Recordings are processed through transcription software and deleted after notes are generated and verified
  • Recordings are not shared with any third party
  • You may request that recording be paused or stopped at any time during a session
I consent to my sessions being audio recorded for clinical documentation purposes
I do not consent to audio recording of my sessions

You may change this preference at any time, including during a session. Opting out will not affect your care.

Surveillance Devices Act 2007 (NSW) s 7 - All-party consent is required for recording private conversations

5 Telehealth Consultations Optional

If telehealth consultations are offered as part of your care:

  • Video/phone consultations are conducted via a secure, encrypted platform
  • Telehealth has limitations - the physiotherapist cannot perform hands-on assessment or treatment
  • You are responsible for ensuring a private, safe environment during the consultation
  • If the physiotherapist determines telehealth is not appropriate, an in-person session will be recommended
  • Telehealth sessions are documented in your clinical record the same as in-person sessions
I consent to telehealth consultations where clinically appropriate
I prefer in-person sessions only

Please indicate how you consent to being contacted regarding your care (appointment reminders, exercise programs, follow-ups):

SMS - I consent to receiving SMS messages about my appointments and care
